Workflow of Wes_Var_call

Raw SRA Data(download.sh)
↓
FASTQ Conversion(download.sh)
↓
Quality Check (fastqc.sh)
↓
Read Trimming (trim.sh)
↓
Reference Preparation(prepare_ref.sh)
↓
Reads Alignment (BWA)[alignemnt.sh]
Sorted BAM Creation  [alignemnt.sh]
↓
Alignment QC[alignment_stats.sh]
↓
WES Metrics(wes_metrics.sh]
↓
Duplicate Marking(mark_duplicate.sh)
↓
Variant Calling[gvcf] (variant_calling.sh)
↓
Combine GVCFs(combine_gvcfs.sh)
↓
Joint Genotyping(genotype_gvcfs.sh)
↓
SNP/INDEL Filtering(filter_snp.sh)/(filter_indels.sh)
↓
Merge Variants(merge_variants.sh)
↓
Variant Annotation(annotation.sh)
↓
Final Annotated Variant Table[variant_table.csv](annotation.sh)

*** to check output files commands are provided in comments in some scripts only where is required.
